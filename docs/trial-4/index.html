How to Play

The 16 words you can see can be divided into 4 groups of 4 words each.

Select four items that belong to the same group/category and tap Submit to check your answer.

You can click on hint to see what the category is.

Find all four groups without making 4 mistakes!

Category Examples

Categories are always specific — not just "climate words" or "things in nature."

Each puzzle has exactly one solution. Watch out for words that seem to fit more than one group!
