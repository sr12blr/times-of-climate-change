Ahmedabad Using Heat Insurance for Informal Workers

Grassroots organizations in Ahmedabad launched parametric heat insurance schemes compensating informal sector women for wage loss during extreme heat days.

Heat Health

What are we talking about?

  • Grassroots organizations in Ahmedabad launched parametric heat insurance schemes compensating informal sector women for wage loss during extreme heat days.
  • When maximum temperatures exceed predefined thresholds, policyholders receive automatic pre-determined payouts — no claim filing required.
  • Example: a Rs 354 annual premium yields Rs 750-1,250 payouts depending on temperature activation.

Why should you care?

  • Informal workers are on the frontlines of extreme heat — they can't work from home or sit in air-conditioned offices. When it's too hot to work, they lose their daily wage.
  • This provides essential safety nets for vulnerable workers facing income loss during dangerous heat, representing a crucial adaptation solution for marginalized communities.
