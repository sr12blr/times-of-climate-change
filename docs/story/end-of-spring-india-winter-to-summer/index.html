The end of Spring: India Jumps Straight from Winter to Summer

Instead of spring, summer arrived overnight. Mumbai was already at 40°C and Shimla 25°C in March.

Heat Food Inflation

What are we talking about?

  • Instead of spring, summer arrived overnight. Mumbai was already at 40°C and Shimla 25°C in March.

Why should you care?

  • Food prices may go up: Wheat needs cool weather before harvest. This year’s heat hit Punjab and Haryana’s crop at exactly that stage, and farmers are predicting a weaker harvest.
  • An El Nino may be coming: Scientists are predicting there’s an 80% chance of El Niño this year, which usually means higher than average temperatures and a weaker monsoon. Dry winter + weak monsoon = water shortages.
